Welcome to our CanSat project

We are the DeltaV team, a dedicated student group from the European Comprehensive School Island Usedom. We have been officially accepted for the German CanSat Competition 2025/26 and are now developing a mini satellite that collects measurement data during rocket flight and transmits it to the ground station. Learn more about our project progress or our team.

What is it about?

A CanSat is a miniature satellite the size of a beverage can. Our mission: collect data, transmit it by radio, and conduct our own scientific experiment ‚Äì space technology in miniature form.

We are the only team from Mecklenburg-Vorpommern participating in the German CanSat Competition 2025/26, and we want to show that space exploration is also possible from the Baltic Sea coast! üåçüöÄ
