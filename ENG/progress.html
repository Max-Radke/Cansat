Concept Development Planned

Development of the primary and secondary missions. Initial technical specifications and material planning.

Design of the circuit diagrams and programming of the sensors. Initial tests with Arduino microcontrollers.

Assembly of all components and extensive tests. Calibration of sensors and software optimization.

Final tests and preparations for the rocket launch. Documentation and presentation preparation.

The big day! Our CanSat will be launched with a rocket and will carry out its missions.

Data Analysis Planned

Analysis of the collected data and preparation of the final report for the competition.
