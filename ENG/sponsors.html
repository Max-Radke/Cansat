Support Our CanSat Project!

🚀 Why Support the DeltaV Team?

As a student team from the Europäische Gesamtschule Insel Usedom, we are participating in the German CanSat Competition 2025/26. We are developing a functional mini-satellite and conducting scientific experiments. Your support helps us explore innovative technologies and gain valuable experience in space technology.

💡 What You Get from a Partnership

📢 Public Relations

Your logo on our website, social media posts, and at presentations

🎓 Talent Development

Support for STEM education and the promotion of technical talent

🏆 Competition Participation

Accompany a real space project from development to launch

📊 Transparency

Regular updates on project progress and use of funds

💰 Sponsorship

🤝 How Can You Support Us?

We welcome different forms of support:

  • Financial Support: For hardware, materials, and travel expenses
  • Donations in Kind: Electronic components, tools, or materials
  • Expert Advice: Expertise in electronics, programming, or project management
  • Public Relations: Help with spreading the word about our project
